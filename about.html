FOOD’S MADE WITH LOVE

Bubba Chinos takes great pride in ensuring every one of their patrons has the best possible experience with every bite of food. Bubba Chinos is all about handmade food crafted with responsibly-sourced, classically-cooked, real food with wholesome ingredients without added colors, flavors or other additives. No matter what time of day it is, no matter what you order, we promise your meal will be top quality and packed with genuine Mexican taste in the way you want it.
